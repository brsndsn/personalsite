Literary Work

I write poetry everyday and am often reading several hours a day. My biggest literary influences are Sylvia Plath, Anne Sexton, and John Steinbeck. My poetry is often of a confessional nature and concerns details of my life and how they seem to manifest in mundane objects of life (nature, chores, etc.) I'm planning on sending this batch of poems I'm writing off for publication (hopefully!) in January 2023.

My favorite novels I have read are The Bell Jar by Sylvia Plath, Of Mice and Men by John Steinbeck, A Raisin in the Sun by Lorraine Hansberry, The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald, and Red Comet by Heather Clark.
